DPS 2019 Holiday Fundraiser

Take care of your holiday shopping and support DPS at the same time!

All orders must be placed by December 7 and must be picked up at the Palo Alto Pet Food Express on Saturday, December 21 or Sunday, December 22 between 12 and 4pm.

DPS is selling hoodie sweatshirts in forest green for $38. A perfect holiday gift for anyone who supports rescue.

DPS is partnering with Rescued Wine to sell soy candles for the holidays for $28 each. Animals and wine lovers unite! Rescued Wine's mission is to give back to animal rescue and better the environment one recycled bottle at a time. Wine bottles are collected, cleaned and cut by hand to create eco-friendly soy candles. Candles are hand-poured in small batches in-house in Truckee, California using artisanal techniques.

Available scents are: Cabernet, Wine for the Holidays, Barrel Aged Whiskey, Clean Home and Fresh Grass. The last two are designed to neutralize pet odor and calm animals.
