My Mutt Program

Let your pup shine in the spotlight AND donate to DPS at the same time!

Pet Food Express' My Mutt program does just that! Donate $250 or more to DPS, and Pet Food Express will arrange a professional photo shoot for your doggie. You choose the final image to be hung in your local Pet Food Express so you can see your pup while you shop.

Also, a huge THANK YOU to all of our supporters who have donated for a MyMutt poster. It always puts a smile on our faces to walk into a PFE and see one of our dogs!
